A LOOK AT THE WATER & SANITATION SERVICE PROVIDERS - SPECIFICALLY IN THE SURVEY AREA

The service providers “prestadores de los servicios de agua y saneamiento” in Mexico are extremely heterogeneous in nature and conditions.

The classification that we use is mostly derived from the Nota Tecnica prepared by some colleagues of the IADB, WSA division (See Riquelme and Martinez 2018, 1–3).
